Jeannette Walls

Jeannette Walls graduated from Barnard College and was a journalist in New York. She is the author of The Silver Star and Half Broke Horses, names one of the ten best books of 2009 ny the editors of the New York Times Book Review. Walls lives in rural Virginia with her husband, the writer John Taylor.
