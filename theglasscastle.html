The Glass Castle

By Jeannette Walls

The Glass Castle tells the story of Jeannette Walls' tumultuous childhood, moving from town to town with her eclectic family. Her father, is constantly uprooting his family to do the skedaddle. Oftentimes, this uprooting is due to evading the law. The book opens with Jeannette's father breaking her out of the hospital without permission from staff or doctors. This hospital visit is prefaced with a disturbing account of Jeannette receiving third-degree burns while trying to make hot dogs (at age 3).
